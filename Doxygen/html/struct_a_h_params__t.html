◆ merger_search_factor

template<class AHFunction >
double AHParams_t< AHFunction >::merger_search_factor

mergers will be searched when distance between 'parent' BHs is distance < merger_search_factor * 4.

  • (AH_initial_guess_1 + AH_initial_guess_2) should be roughly '2M=2(m1+m2)' for initial guess at m/2 (set to non-positive to 'always search')
